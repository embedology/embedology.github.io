Services

  • Electronic system design - Embedology can help you form a specification for your requirements, then design the electronics and / or write the embedded software to fulfil those requirements.

  • Cloud systems - Designing applications for large scale operations at internet scale.

  • Medical Electronics - Embedology has been involved with significant medical electronics projects and the extended product quality management systems requirements.

  • Obsolete equipment / Reverse engineering - Have you a piece of capital equipment which has reached the end of its life because a board or component is no longer available? We can help you re-engineer the missing component.

  • PCB design - We can design and / or supply PCBs to your schematics.

  • Database design / Database conversion - Do you have information in an obsolete database which needs to be transferred to your new database system? We can generate cost effective solutions which are economical for only a few hundred records rather than manually re-keying data.

  • Interfacing / Data communications - Need to interface anything to a PC or the internet? APIs, Ethernet, USB. We can produce cost-effective solutions for your connection problems.

  • Test equipment - We can help you develop a cost-effective test rig for automated testing. If it can be tested electronically, then we can design this for you and help you speed up your production.

  • Product or Process Assessments - Has your product development ground to a halt? We can meet you to discuss a way ahead. It sometimes helps to brainstorm with like-minded individuals - and get their help to restart the momentum.
